Know About Us

Who We Are

We believe, mental health is as important as physical health and people need to be aware of mental health issues and how to deal with them. 7.5% of Indians suffer from mental disorders and yet, so many cases go unreported and are ignored in the name of taboo.

According to National Mental Health Survey of India, every sixth Indian needs mental health help and how many of them actually go to seek professional help? The problem stays ignored unless its symptoms reaches a threshold.

Started as an initiative by Psychology students in 2018, Pehel is dedicated towards spreading the word among general community of India to break religious barriers and fear among people to make the country mentally fit.

Connecting to Help

Providing a network of expert psychologists, tele-services and online consultancies to keep the happiness never-lasting.

Promoting Mental Health

Spreading the word on the importance of mental health through camps and various events throughout country.

Building a Network

Constructing a network of psychologists and mental health experts ensuring no one walk away without help.

Get Help

Contacting a psychologist or seeking help doesn't mean you are mad. Let your emotions come out and seek the help you deserve without any fear.
